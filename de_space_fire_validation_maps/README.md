# 🌾 RAP 10 m Cover — Fire Validation Maps

Interactive maps validating **Rangeland Analysis Platform (RAP) 10 m** vegetation
fractional cover extracted around building/structure points, for the Carlson
fire-validation work (de_space paper). Each map has an Esri World Imagery basemap,
toggleable per-band RAP cover overlays (AFG, BGR, LTR, PFG, SHR, TRE; cover "lights
up" over imagery), and structure points colored by tree (TRE) 40 m mean cover.

> ⚠️ These are validation artifacts from a different project than the WRI index; they
> live here only for shareable rendering. RAP is **not meaningful over developed/
> impervious surfaces** — interpret urban cores with caution.

**Bands:** `AFG` annual forb & grass · `BGR` bare ground · `LTR` litter ·
`PFG` perennial forb & grass · `SHR` shrub · `TRE` tree (all % cover, 0–100).

**Source:** RAP 10 m `pft` product (Allred et al. 2025, *Scientific Data* 12:1889; CC BY 4.0),
streamed from NTSG COGs. Generated by `carlson_fire_validation_scripts/01h_validate_rap_cover_maps.py`.
